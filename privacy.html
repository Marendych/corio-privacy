Privacy Policy

FORMA

Last updated: April 25, 2026

Forma ("we", "our", or "the app") is a running and recovery tracking application for iOS. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

Account information

  • Email address (used for authentication)
  • Display name and username (optional, set by you)
  • Profile photo (optional, uploaded by you)

Health & fitness data (via Apple HealthKit)

  • Heart rate and resting heart rate
  • Sleep analysis
  • Step count
  • Workout sessions
  • VO2 max (when available)

HealthKit data is used only within the app to calculate your recovery, readiness, and training metrics. It is never shared with advertisers or third parties for commercial purposes.

Wearable device data (via Oura API)

  • Readiness score, sleep score, HRV, resting heart rate
  • Sleep stages (hypnogram), body temperature
  • Stress balance

This data is fetched from Oura's servers using your personal OAuth token and is stored on our secure backend (Supabase) solely to power your dashboard and analytics.

Location data

  • GPS coordinates during active runs (for route tracking, distance, and pace)

Location is collected only when you start a run. We do not track your location in the background outside of active workouts.

Usage data

  • Run records you create (distance, duration, route, heart rate zones)
  • Shoes you add and their mileage
  • App settings and preferences

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Display your personalized recovery dashboard (readiness, sleep, HRV)
  • Calculate training load, form score, and recommendations
  • Show your run history, statistics, and analytics
  • Enable social features (friends, squad leaderboard, run feed) — only with people you choose to follow
  • Send push notifications you have opted into (morning briefing, workout reminders)
  • Sync data between your devices via our secure backend

We do not sell your data. We do not use your health data for advertising.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase infrastructure (hosted in the EU / US). All data in transit is encrypted via TLS. Authentication tokens are stored in the iOS Keychain. HealthKit data is processed on-device and only the derived metrics (scores, averages) are synced to our servers — raw HealthKit samples never leave your device.

4. Data Sharing

  • Friends you add — they can see your readiness score, sleep score, and recent runs if you enable sharing in Settings → Privacy.
  • Oura — we access your Oura data via OAuth on your behalf. We do not share your data back with Oura beyond what their API requires.
  • Apple — if you use Sign in with Apple, Apple processes your authentication. We receive only your email (or a relay address).
  • No advertising networks — we do not integrate any ad SDKs or sell data to advertisers.

5. Apple HealthKit

Forma accesses Apple HealthKit data only with your explicit permission. You can revoke HealthKit access at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Forma.

HealthKit data is used exclusively to provide your in-app experience. It will never be shared with third parties, used for advertising, or sold.

6. Push Notifications

We may send you push notifications for: morning recovery briefing, workout reminders, and social interactions (friend requests, kudos). You can manage or disable notifications at any time in Settings → Notifications within Forma, or in iOS Settings → Forma → Notifications.

7. Your Rights

  • Access — you can view all data we store about you in the app.
  • Deletion — you can delete your account and all associated data from Settings → Account → Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data from our servers.
  • Export — your run data can be exported in the app (Settings → Export Data).
  • Opt-out — you can disable health data sharing with friends at any time in Settings → Privacy.

8. Children's Privacy

Forma is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us to have it removed.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via a notification in the app. Continued use of Forma after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
